My journey towards User Experience

Like many, I have discovered and experimented a lot with the tools that came along with the digital age!, I first discovered my passion for UX design during my front-end developer internship at Pinesphere Solutions. Around the same time, I also started as a graphic designer at Bright Bridge Info Tech. This role allowed me to gain valuable experience in visual communication and using design tools for marketing materials. The combination of front-end development exposure and hands-on graphic design work got me really excited about the field of UX.

Working closely with UX designers and seeing how wireframes translate into actual UI code opened my eyes to the impact of user-centric design. I was fascinated with how even small tweaks could really improve the overall user experience. and I especially enjoyed messing around with music.

This motivated me to pursue formal UX/UI design training. I completed an intensive certification course at Designerrs Academy, where I learned core skills like user research, information architecture, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing. With this knowledge, I felt equipped to take on UX projects. Eager to apply my new skills, I took on two major UX/UI projects in 2022. For a travel planning app and a home services app, I independently handled the entire design process from research to high-fidelity prototyping. Refining the user journeys through iterative testing was so rewarding. This experience made me realize I wanted to specialize in UX design long-term.

To further boost my expertise, I decided to pursue a master's degree in User Experience Design at Edinburgh Napier University. This program will give me advanced skills and a strong portfolio to become an expert in the field. Learning UX design formally at the master's level is the perfect next step for me.

In the meantime, I'm getting valuable visual communication experience in my current role as a graphic designer. This allows me to improve my UI design abilities while also learning new tools and techniques. The combined graphic design and UX knowledge makes me a well-rounded designer. I'm proud of the path I've taken so far to systematically build up my UX skills - from that first internship to hands-on projects to formal education. With passion and dedication, I'm on track to becoming a UX expert and can't wait to see where this journey takes me next!
